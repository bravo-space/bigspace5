The opportunity
Web3 communities interact in a completely different ecosystem than online communities of the past, but the managers and creators of such communities often need to fly blind - there is limited data, analytics, and relationship management infrastructure for this new stack of both on-chain and off-chain information. Boolean Labs is building a community analytics platform to help creators better understand, nurture, and organize their communities.
About Boolean Labs
Boolean Labs is a web3 venture studio that builds new technology businesses from scratch in the world of crypto, defi, NFTs, and web3 generally. Boolean brings together de-risked ideas with experienced operators and the capital and operational resources they need to grow and scale. Boolean Labs is a collaboration between Diagram Ventures, a $200M venture studio based out of Montreal, and StreamingFast, a team of web3 builders, investors, and core developers of The Graph protocol.
